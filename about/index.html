About SelfTalk

We believe everyone has the inner power to change. Through scientific self-dialogue training, we help you reshape habits and achieve transformation.

Our Story

2020

The Beginning

Our founding team discovered in psychological research that self-dialogue is one of the most effective methods for behavior change. We started thinking: how can we help more people master this skill?

2021

Theory Validation

We deeply studied cognitive behavioral therapy, mindfulness meditation and other scientific methods, combining them with Chinese cultural background to develop a self-dialogue training system suitable for Chinese people.

2022

Product Launch

SelfTalk platform officially launched, with the first batch of users starting their 21-day transformation journey. We were delighted to find that over 70% of users successfully established new habits.

2023 - Present

Continuous Growth

Helping tens of thousands of users successfully change their habits, continuously optimizing product experience, committed to being your most caring habit change partner.
