About Me

I used to get lost in live streams and short videos, but a self-talk routine helped me quit in just a few weeks. Now I'm sharing the exact practice that worked for me and the people around me.

My Story

2021

Stuck In Endless Scrolling

I spent late nights hopping between live streams and short videos, riding emotional highs and lows I couldn't control. Journaling helped me notice my inner dialogue was the real key to change.

2022

The SelfTalk Method Took Shape

I turned cognitive-behavior tools into daily prompts. By repeating them, the cravings to scroll faded, and I reclaimed my focus in just a few weeks.

2023

Friends Saw Change

I invited friends to try the same steps; one quit smoking, another walked away from social drinking. Together we learned how powerful consistent self-talk can be.

2024 - Present

Sharing It With You

I packaged everything into SelfTalk so anyone working to break a habit can find a practical, compassionate guide.
